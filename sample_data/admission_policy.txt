Greenwood International School - Admissions Policy and Process Guidelines

1. Introduction
Welcome to Greenwood International School, where we foster academic excellence and global leadership. This policy guide outlines the rules, step-by-step processes, and documents required to secure an enrollment for your child.

2. Step-by-Step Admission Process
- Phase 1: Online Registration: Complete the inquiry form on our ERP portal and upload the student profile.
- Phase 2: Document Verification: The admissions desk will audit academic records and birth certificates.
- Phase 3: Interaction and Evaluation:
  - Kindergarten applicants will undergo a non-stressful play-based interactive interview.
  - Grades 1 through 5 applicants participate in a basic English and Mathematics assessment.
  - Grades 6 through 10 participate in a comprehensive Cognitive and Scientific evaluation.
- Phase 4: Fee Submission: Pay the registration fee within 10 business days of selection to guarantee the seat.

3. Mandatory Documents Checklist
All parents must submit clear scanned copies of:
- Government-issued birth certificate
- Previous 2 years of scholastic report cards/transcripts
- Official Transfer Certificate (TC) or Leaving Certificate from the previous school
- Passport-sized student photo with white background (4 copies)
- National Identity Card of parents (Aadhaar Card, Passport, or State ID)

4. Age Criteria for Eligibility (As of June 1st of the Academic Year)
- Nursery: 3 years+
- LKG (Lower Kindergarten): 4 years+
- UKG (Upper Kindergarten): 5 years+
- Grade 1: 6 years+
- Grade 5: 10 years+
- Grade 10: 15 years+

5. Important Admission Timelines (Session 2026-2027)
- Online Application Opens: January 15, 2026
- Last Date for Regular Registrations: August 15, 2026
- Entrance Assessment Schedule: Conducted weekly on Wednesday mornings.
- Session Starts: September 1, 2026
